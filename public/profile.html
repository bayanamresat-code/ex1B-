Mickey the Debug Dog

Powers

As Douglas Adams once said "One is never alone with a rubber duck." Mickey, the Debug Dog is a patient listener, able to help you solve your debugging woes. Unlike a rubber duck, Mickey is also soft and cuddly, doubling as a pillow for those occasional times you need to just close your eyes for a 5 minute power nap.
